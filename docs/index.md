Welcome to **GlennMurray Published (GLMR)**, a lecture-note style journal for the Introduction to AI course. Our current issue, **Searching Strategy and Its Application**, assembles every midterm presentation and report so the class can archive, reference, and celebrate their work.

### Highlights

<ul class="callout-list">
  <li><strong>Comprehensive Coverage:</strong> Eight research reports and ten presentation decks spanning routing, robotics, logistics, tourism, and game AI.</li>
  <li><strong>Top Scholarship:</strong> Group 6’s maze pathfinding report leads with a weighted score of 9.12, closely followed by Group 1’s resilient routing study at 8.66.</li>
  <li><strong>Immediate Access:</strong> Every submission features inline viewing via PDF streaming plus downloadable archives for offline review.</li>
</ul>
